# Landing Page Truthfulness Audit Backlog

Date: 2026-07-09
Scope: `landing/index.html`
Status: required before deployment or sending outreach traffic

## Why this audit is required

The landing page was written before MMTVU had validated customer outcomes. It currently contains scarcity, revenue-loss, timing, and guarantee claims that may not be backed by operating evidence. Pre-revenue positioning must distinguish a hypothesis or founding-pilot offer from proven customer results.

## Claims requiring evidence or revision

1. `$47K/year` leaked-revenue claim in the page metadata.
   - Document a defensible source and applicability to the target ICP, or replace it with non-quantified language.

2. `3 pilot spots left for July` / `3 pilot spots remaining for July 2026`.
   - Confirm this reflects a real capacity constraint and update/remove it when the month or capacity changes.

3. `free 14-day pilot` and `No credit card required`.
   - Confirm these are the approved commercial terms and that the fulfillment process can support them.

4. Full-refund guarantee, including the setup fee, if the client is not booking more jobs within 30 days.
   - Define attribution, eligibility, exclusions, refund mechanics, and operational authority before publishing.

5. `By day 3` leads will be followed up and `By day 14` jobs will have been booked.
   - Treat these as unproven outcome guarantees unless backed by real pilot evidence. Replace with process commitments where appropriate.

6. Any testimonial, customer-count, logo, expert-background, conversion-rate, or case-study claim.
   - Publish only with artifact-backed evidence and permission. Do not use hypothetical examples as live claims.

## Required review before deployment

- Verify every numerical claim against a primary source or MMTVU evidence artifact.
- Verify every scarcity statement against actual delivery capacity and a real expiration condition.
- Reconcile pricing and pilot terms with the current approved offer.
- Replace outcome guarantees with bounded process commitments unless fulfillment evidence supports them.
- Confirm cancellation, refund, privacy, communications-consent, and data-handling language.
- Run CEO-style review and conversion-copy review after truthfulness corrections.

## Safe pre-revenue framing

Prefer language such as:

- `Founding pilot for home-service teams that want a more consistent estimate follow-up process.`
- `We will configure and operate the agreed follow-up workflow during the pilot.`
- `The pilot is designed to measure response, discovery, proposal, and paid-conversion evidence; results are not guaranteed.`
- `Founding-pilot terms are available only while MMTVU has verified onboarding capacity.`

This file records a separate landing-page workstream. It must not be mixed into the Evidence Loop implementation commit.
