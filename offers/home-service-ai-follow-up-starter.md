# Offer: Home Service AI Follow-Up Starter

## One-Line Pitch

We install an AI follow-up layer for home service companies so your office team stops losing leads and chasing quotes manually.

## Target Customer

Home service businesses with office staff:

- HVAC companies
- Plumbers
- Roofers
- Electricians
- Remodelers
- Pest control
- Garage door companies

Best first-fit customer:
A local company already getting quote requests, already paying office staff, but without consistent follow-up after the first call or estimate.

## Pain

The business is not necessarily missing every call. The real pain is that follow-up is inconsistent and expensive:

- Staff responds when they have time.
- Quote follow-up depends on memory.
- Leads go cold after one attempt.
- Old estimates are rarely reactivated.
- Owners do not know which leads were actually chased.
- Office staff spends too much time on repetitive nudges.

## Promise

When a lead or quote request comes in, the system follows up quickly, asks basic qualifying questions, keeps nudging for a defined period, and gives staff a daily list of hot leads that need human attention.

## Starter Package

### Setup

- Map current lead sources
- Write follow-up scripts
- Configure SMS/email automations
- Create quote follow-up sequence
- Create stale lead reactivation sequence
- Create daily owner/staff summary
- Run 30-day optimization pass

### Not Included Initially

- Full CRM rebuild
- Paid ads
- Complex voice AI
- Custom SaaS dashboard
- Staff replacement
- Deep custom integrations

## Price Test

Primary test:

- $500 setup
- $300/month

Alternate tests:

- $0 setup + $500/month
- $1,000 setup + $250/month

## Discovery Question

Ask owners:

> When a quote request comes in, how many times does your team follow up before giving up?

Follow-up question:

> What happens to leads that do not book on the first call?

If pain appears:

> If I could set up an automated follow-up system that chased those leads for you and gave your office staff a daily hot-lead list, would that be worth testing?

## Success Criteria For First Pilot

A pilot is successful if it produces at least one of:

- More booked estimate appointments
- Faster response time
- More followed-up quotes
- Less manual staff follow-up
- Owner says the daily hot-lead list is useful
- One testimonial or measurable before/after result


## V0 Delivery Stack

- Google Sheets or Airtable for lead tracking
- Make.com or Zapier for automation
- Gmail/Google Workspace or SendGrid for email
- Twilio for SMS only when consent is explicit
- Google Calendar or existing booking link
- Daily summary by email

V0 does not replace the client's CRM. Start with one capture mode: website form forwarding, quote-request email forwarding, or manual staff entry.

## Follow-Up Cadence

- Immediate acknowledgement
- 2-hour nudge
- 1-day nudge
- 3-day nudge
- 7-day close-the-loop message
- Quote follow-up at 1, 3, 7, and optionally 14 days

## Compliance Rules

- Client approves scripts before go-live
- SMS only with consent or expected service-request follow-up
- STOP/HELP handling required for SMS
- Avoid emergency, legal, angry, warranty, or sensitive cases
- Keep message and opt-out logs
- Send during reasonable business hours

## Pilot Types

- Discovery only: unpaid workflow interview
- Free pilot: 14 days, one lead source, one success metric
- Paid pilot: discounted setup with monthly continuation
- Full setup: $500 setup + $300/month price test
