### 2. Home Service AI Follow-Up

Role:
First active cashflow engine.

Offer:
AI follow-up systems for home service businesses with office staff.

Target customers:
Plumbers, HVAC companies, roofers, electricians, and similar local operators.

Core promise:
Office teams stop manually chasing every lead. The AI system follows up instantly, nudges quotes, and gives staff a daily hot-lead list.

## Business Thesis

Affiliate/media assets are useful but slow. The first active business should be a productized service that can produce cashflow before large traffic exists.

MMTVUEntertainment should use AI to build, operate, and compound income streams:

1. Productized AI services
2. Media/affiliate sites
3. Digital products and templates
4. Automation/resale offers
5. Eventually small SaaS or internal software assets

## Immediate Focus

The current focus is selling and validating the Home Service AI Follow-Up offer.

Next assignment:
Contact 10 home service businesses and ask:

> When a quote request comes in, how many times does your team follow up before giving up?

If they describe inconsistent follow-up, slow staff, missed quotes, or stale leads, ask:

> If I could set up an automated follow-up system that chased those leads for you and gave your office staff a daily hot-lead list, would that be worth testing?
